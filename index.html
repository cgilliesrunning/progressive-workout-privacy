Privacy Policy for Progressive Workout

Last updated: 9 July 2026

Progressive Workout (“the app”, “we”, “us”) is a workout-tracking app built around progressive overload. This policy explains what data the app collects, why, and your choices. It applies to the iOS and Android versions of the app.

Who is responsible for your data

The app is developed and operated by Cameron Gillies. For any privacy questions or requests, contact cgilliesrunning@gmail.com.

What we collect

We only collect what the app needs to function:

  • Account identity. When you sign in with email, Google, or Apple, we store your account identifier and email address via Firebase Authentication. This lets your data survive reinstalls and sync across devices.
  • Your workout data. Programs, exercises, workout sessions, logged sets, and your app settings (units, available weights, rest-timer defaults, rep ranges, warm-ups and cardio). This is stored in Google Cloud Firestore under your account.
  • Crash diagnostics. If the app crashes, Firebase Crashlytics records a crash report (device model, OS version, and a stack trace) so we can fix bugs. These reports do not identify you personally.

We do not collect analytics or advertising data, and the app contains no ads.

Health data

If you enable the Apple Health / Google Health Connect integration, the app writes a workout entry (activity type: strength training, with start and end time) to your health app after you complete a session. This is optional, off by default, and can be turned off at any time in Settings. The app does not read your health data or transmit it to us.

How your data is used

Your data is used solely to operate the app: to show your programs and progress, apply progression rules, generate your reports, and keep everything synced to your account. We do not sell your data or share it with third parties for marketing.

Where your data is stored

Data is stored using Google Firebase (Authentication, Cloud Firestore, and Crashlytics), operated by Google. Google processes this data on our behalf as a service provider. See Google’s privacy policy at https://policies.google.com/privacy.

Data retention and deletion

Your data is kept while your account exists. You can delete your account from within the app (Settings → Account), which permanently removes your profile, programs, exercises, and workout history, and deletes your authentication record. Deletion is immediate and cannot be undone. You may also email us to request deletion.

Children

The app is not directed at children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect data from them.

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Material changes will be reflected by updating the “Last updated” date above.
