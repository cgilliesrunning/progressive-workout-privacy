Delete your Progressive Workout account & data

Last updated: 9 July 2026

This page explains how to delete your Progressive Workout account and all of the data associated with it. It applies to the app published by Cameron Gillies under the package name com.cgillies.workout.

Option 1 — Delete in the app (immediate)

  1. Open Progressive Workout and sign in.
  2. Go to Settings (gear icon, top right).
  3. Scroll to Account and tap Delete account.
  4. Confirm (you may be asked to re-enter your password or sign in again).

This immediately and permanently deletes your account and all associated data.

Option 2 — Request deletion by email

If you can’t access the app, email cgilliesrunning@gmail.com from the address associated with your account, with the subject “Delete my Progressive Workout account.” We will delete your account and data within 30 days and confirm by reply.

What is deleted

All data tied to your account is permanently removed, including:

  • Your account identity (email / sign-in record).
  • Your programs, days, exercises, warm-ups and cardio.
  • Your workout sessions and logged sets.
  • Your app settings (units, available weights, rest defaults, rep ranges).

Deletion is permanent and cannot be undone. Crash diagnostics, which do not identify you personally, are retained only as long as needed to fix bugs and are not linked to your account after deletion.
